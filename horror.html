The Shining Poster

The Shining

1980

Main Cast:

  • Jack Nicholson as Jack Torrance
  • Shelley Duvall as Wendy Torrance
  • Danny Lloyd as Danny Torrance

A family becomes caretakers of an isolated hotel where the father slowly descends into violent madness. Stanley Kubrick's adaptation of Stephen King's novel is a masterclass in atmospheric horror and psychological tension. Jack Nicholson's iconic performance and the film's innovative cinematography have made it one of the most studied horror films. The Overlook Hotel itself becomes a character through Kubrick's meticulous direction and haunting imagery.

Get Out Poster

Get Out

2017

Main Cast:

  • Daniel Kaluuya as Chris Washington
  • Allison Williams as Rose Armitage
  • Bradley Whitford as Dean Armitage

A young Black man uncovers disturbing secrets when he meets his white girlfriend's family. Jordan Peele's directorial debut blends social commentary with horror to create a groundbreaking thriller. The film earned four Oscar nominations including Best Picture and won for Best Original Screenplay.

Hereditary Poster

Hereditary

2018

Main Cast:

  • Toni Collette as Annie Graham
  • Alex Wolff as Peter Graham
  • Milly Shapiro as Charlie Graham

A family grapples with disturbing occurrences following the death of their secretive grandmother. Ari Aster's debut delivers relentless psychological horror through exceptional performances and disturbing imagery. Toni Collette's powerhouse performance anchors this tale of grief, trauma, and occult terror.

The Conjuring Poster

The Conjuring

2013

Main Cast:

  • Patrick Wilson as Ed Warren
  • Vera Farmiga as Lorraine Warren
  • Lili Taylor as Carolyn Perron

Paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse. James Wan crafted a modern horror classic that revitalized the supernatural genre. The film's success spawned a massive franchise known as The Conjuring Universe.

A Quiet Place Poster

A Quiet Place

2018

Main Cast:

  • Emily Blunt as Evelyn Abbott
  • John Krasinski as Lee Abbott
  • Millicent Simmonds as Regan Abbott

A family must live in silence while hiding from creatures that hunt by sound. John Krasinski's directorial effort creates intense suspense through innovative sound design and minimal dialogue. The film demonstrates how limitations can enhance creativity in horror filmmaking.

The Exorcist Poster

The Exorcist

1973

Main Cast:

  • Ellen Burstyn as Chris MacNeil
  • Linda Blair as Regan MacNeil
  • Max von Sydow as Father Merrin

Two priests attempt to exorcise a demonic entity possessing a young girl. William Friedkin's horror masterpiece became a cultural phenomenon and redefined the genre. The film was the first horror movie nominated for Best Picture and remains terrifying decades later.
